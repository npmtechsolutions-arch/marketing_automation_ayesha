```markdown
**"I don't have a Facebook Business Page"**
- Create one first: [facebook.com/pages/create](https://www.facebook.com/pages/create)
- Takes 2 minutes

**"I'm not an admin of the page"**
- Ask the page owner to add you as admin
- Go to Page Settings → Page Roles

**"Instagram isn't showing up"**
- Make sure your Instagram is a Business/Creator account
- Convert in Instagram app: Settings → Account → Switch to Professional Account
- Link it to your Facebook Page: Settings → Linked Accounts

**"Connection failed"**
- Try logging out of Facebook and back in
- Clear your browser cache
- Make sure pop-ups are allowed
- Contact support if still stuck

## What We Can Do
✓ Post to your Facebook Page
✓ Post to your Instagram account
✓ See basic analytics (likes, comments, shares)

## What We Cannot Do
✗ Post to your personal Facebook profile
✗ Read your private messages
✗ Access your personal data
```

#### Understanding Your Dashboard
```markdown
# Understanding Your Analytics Dashboard

## Key Metrics Overview

**Reach:**
- How many people saw your posts
- Higher reach = more visibility
- Doesn't mean they engaged

**Engagement:**
- Likes + Comments + Shares + Saves
- Shows people interacting with your content
- Most important metric

**Engagement Rate:**
- (Engagement / Reach) × 100
- Industry average: 1-3%
- Higher is better

**Follower Growth:**
- New followers this period
- Track over time
- Quality > quantity

**Click-Through Rate (CTR):**
- % of people who clicked your link
- Only for posts with links
- Important for driving traffic

**Best Performing Post:**
- Your top post this period
- Learn what worked
- Create similar content

## Reading Your Performance Report

**Top Section: Overview Cards**
```
┌─────────────────┐  ┌─────────────────┐
│   Total Reach   │  │  Engagement     │
│      45,231     │  │      2,847      │
│   +15% ↑        │  │   +23% ↑        │
└─────────────────┘  └─────────────────┘
```

- Percentage = change from previous period
- ↑ Green = improving
- ↓ Red = declining

- Line graph showing trends
- Hover for exact numbers
- Compare platforms
- Spot patterns (best days/times)
```

```markdown
# A/B Testing Your Content

## What is A/B Testing?

Test two versions of the same post to see which performs better:
- Version A: Original caption
- Version B: Different caption/image/time

Winner gets used more in future posts.

## How to A/B Test

1. **Create Your Post**
   - Generate or write your content

2. **Click "Create A/B Test"**
   - System creates Version B

3. **Make ONE Change:**
   - Different headline
   - Different image
   - Different call-to-action
   - Different posting time

**Important:** Only change ONE thing so you know what made the difference

4. **Set Test Duration:**
   - Minimum: 3 days
   - Recommended: 7 days
   - Both versions post to same platform

5. **Review Results:**
   - After test period, see winner
   - Higher engagement = winner
   - AI automatically applies learnings

## What to A/B Test

**Good Tests:**
- Caption with emoji vs without emoji
- Question in caption vs statement
- "Buy now" CTA vs "Learn more" CTA
- Post at 9 AM vs 1 PM
- Lifestyle image vs product image
- Short caption vs long caption

**Bad Tests:** (Don't test these)
- Completely different topics
- Changing 3+ things at once
- Different platforms (unfair comparison)
```

+57% more engagement

```markdown
# Content Best Practices

## General Social Media Tips

### Posting Frequency

**Instagram:**
- 4-7 posts per week
- 1-2 Stories per day
- Consistency > Quantity

**Facebook:**
- 3-5 posts per week
- Mix of content types
- Don't over-post (hurts reach)

**LinkedIn:**
- 2-3 posts per week
- Quality over quantity
- Professional content only

**Twitter/X:**
- 3-5 posts per day
- Short and punchy
- Engage with trending topics

### Content Mix (60-30-10 Rule)

**60% Educational/Valuable**
- Tips, how-tos, insights
- Industry news and trends
- Behind-the-scenes
- Customer stories

**30% Engaging/Entertaining**
- Memes (if brand-appropriate)
- Questions
- Polls
- User-generated content

**10% Promotional**
- Product features
- Sales announcements
- Special offers
- "Buy now" CTAs

**Why:** People don't follow brands for ads. Provide value first.

### Writing Captions

**Good Caption Formula:**
1. **Hook** (first line grabs attention)
2. **Value** (useful information or story)
3. **Call-to-Action** (what should they do?)

**Examples:**

❌ Bad: "Check out our new product. Link in bio."

✅ Good: "Spent 6 months creating this. Here's what makes it different: [value props]. Ready to try it? Link in bio 👆"

**Tips:**
- Start with a question or bold statement
- Use emojis (but don't overdo it)
- Break into short paragraphs (easier to read)
- Include CTA at the end
- Tell stories, not sales pitches

### Hashtag Strategy

**Instagram:**
- Use 5-10 hashtags
- Mix popular + niche
- Example: #marketing (popular) + #b2bsaastips (niche)
- Place in first comment (looks cleaner)

**LinkedIn:**
- Use 3-5 hashtags
- Professional, industry-specific
- Example: #leadership #productmanagement #startups

**Twitter/X:**
- Use 1-2 hashtags max
- Only trending or branded hashtags
- More hashtags = lower engagement

**Finding Good Hashtags:**
1. Check what competitors use
2. Use our AI suggestions
3. Search hashtags to see popularity
4. Track which ones drive engagement for you

### Visual Content Tips

**Images:**
- High quality (not pixelated)
- Good lighting
- Clear subject
- Brand colors when possible
- Text overlay for quotes/stats

**Videos:**
- First 3 seconds matter most
- Add captions (80% watch without sound)
- Keep under 60 seconds
- Show faces (performs better)

**Aspect Ratios:**
- Instagram Feed: 1:1 (square)
- Instagram Stories: 9:16 (vertical)
- Facebook: 16:9 or 1:1
- LinkedIn: 1.91:1 (landscape) or 1:1

### Engagement Tactics

**Ask Questions:**
- "What's your biggest challenge with X?"
- "Team A or Team B?"
- "What would you add to this list?"

**Use Calls-to-Action:**
- "Save this for later"
- "Share with someone who needs this"
- "Tag a friend who [relevant]"
- "Double-tap if you agree"

**Respond to Comments:**
- Reply within 1 hour (if possible)
- Ask follow-up questions
- Like comments (shows appreciation)
- Creates community feeling

## Platform-Specific Tips

### Instagram

**Best Times:** Weekdays 11 AM, 1 PM, evenings 7-9 PM
**Best Days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday

**What Works:**
- Reels (pushed by algorithm)
- Carousels (swipe posts)
- Stories with polls/questions
- Behind-the-scenes content

**What Doesn't:**
- Over-filtered photos
- Stock images (too generic)
- Link spam
- Buying followers (hurts engagement)

### LinkedIn

**Best Times:** Weekday mornings 8-9 AM, lunch 12-1 PM
**Best Days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday

**What Works:**
- Thought leadership
- Industry insights
- Company culture posts
- Professional achievements
- How-to guides

**What Doesn't:**
- Memes (mostly)
- Overly casual content
- Hard sales pitches
- Personal drama
- Political rants

### Facebook

**Best Times:** Weekdays 9 AM-12 PM, evenings 7-8 PM
**Best Days:** Wednesday-Friday

**What Works:**
- Video content
- Live videos
- Community engagement
- Events
- Local content (if local business)

**What Doesn't:**
- Click-bait
- Link spam
- Engagement bait ("Like if you agree!")
- Too many posts per day

### Twitter/X

**Best Times:** Weekdays 9 AM, 12 PM, 8 PM
**Best Days:** Monday-Friday

**What Works:**
- Trending topics
- Quick insights
- Thread (series of tweets)
- Polls
- Engaging with others

**What Doesn't:**
- Long paragraphs
- Too many hashtags
- Spamming
- Ignoring replies
```

```markdown
# Growing Your Audience

## Organic Growth Strategies

### 1. Consistent Posting
- Post at least 3-4 times per week
- Same days/times (train your audience)
- Don't disappear for weeks

### 2. Engage with Others
- Comment on competitor posts
- Reply to industry influencers
- Join relevant conversations
- Be helpful, not promotional

### 3. Use Strategic Hashtags
- Research popular hashtags in your niche
- Mix popular + niche
- Track which ones bring followers

### 4. Collaborate
- Partner with complementary brands
- Guest post exchanges
- Shout-out trades
- Co-host live sessions

### 5. Optimize Your Profile
- Clear bio (what you do)
- Professional photo
- Link to website
- Call-to-action in bio

### 6. Create Shareable Content
- Infographics
- Quick tips
- Quotes
- Memes (if appropriate)
- Controversial opinions (carefully)

### 7. Run Contests/Giveaways
- "Follow + Tag 3 friends to enter"
- Increases reach quickly
- Make sure prize is relevant (attracts right audience)

## What NOT to Do

❌ **Buy Followers**
- Fake accounts
- Hurts engagement rate
- Platforms penalize this
- Waste of money

❌ **Follow/Unfollow Tactic**
- Annoying
- Unsustainable
- Low-quality followers
- Platforms ban for this

❌ **Engagement Pods**
- Fake engagement
- Doesn't help algorithm long-term
- Wastes time

❌ **Spam Comments**
- "Great post! Check out my page!"
- Everyone ignores these
- Looks desperate

## Paid Growth (When Ready)

### Facebook/Instagram Ads
**When:** Have consistent organic content first
**Budget:** Start with $5-10/day
**Goal:** Page likes, post engagement, website traffic

### LinkedIn Ads
**When:** B2B with higher budget
**Budget:** $100/day minimum (expensive)
**Goal:** Lead generation, brand awareness

### Influencer Marketing
**When:** Have clear product-market fit
**Find:** Micro-influencers (10K-100K followers)
**Cost:** $100-$1000 per post (varies widely)

## Tracking Growth

**Measure:**
- Follower count (but not the only metric)
- Engagement rate (more important)
- Website clicks
- Profile visits
- DMs and inquiries

**Set Goals:**
- Example: "Grow from 500 to 2,000 followers in 3 months"
- Break down: +500/month = +16-17/day
- Track weekly in our analytics

**Adjust Strategy:**
- What content brings followers?
- Which platforms grow fastest?
- Double down on what works
- Cut what doesn't

## Remember

**Quality > Quantity:**
- 1,000 engaged followers > 10,000 ghost followers
- Focus on people who will buy/engage
- Purge fake/inactive followers periodically
```
