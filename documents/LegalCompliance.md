## 🚨 Incident Response Plan

### Data Breach Protocol

**1. Detection & Containment (0-1 hour)**
- Identify source of breach
- Stop ongoing breach
- Secure systems
- Document everything

**2. Assessment (1-24 hours)**
- Determine scope: what data was accessed?
- How many users affected?
- Was data encrypted?
- Likelihood of harm to users

**3. Notification (24-72 hours)**
- Notify affected users (ASAP)
- Notify authorities if required:
  - EU: Within 72 hours (GDPR)
  - US: Varies by state
- Notify cyber insurance (if applicable)

**4. Remediation**
- Fix vulnerability
- Change credentials
- Update security measures
- Conduct post-mortem

**5. Documentation**
- Timeline of events
- Actions taken
- Lessons learned
- Policy updates

### Who to Contact

**Internal:**
- CTO/Engineering Lead
- Legal Counsel
- PR/Communications
- Customer Support

**External:**
- Data Protection Authority (EU)
- State Attorney General (US)
- FBI (if criminal activity)
- Cyber insurance provider
- Affected users

**Contact Information:**
Keep updated list with phone numbers and emails.
